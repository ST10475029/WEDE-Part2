Our Story

Founded in 2023 in Johannesburg, SheStrong began as a grassroots initiative to create a safe, empowering space for women to pursue fitness and wellness.

What started as weekend bootcamps in local parks quickly grew into a movement. Today, SheStrong is a vibrant community of women who uplift one another through strength training, mindfulness, and shared purpose.

Mission

To empower women through fitness, fostering strength, self-love, and community.

We believe that every woman deserves access to wellness tools that honor her body, mind, and spirit. Our mission is to break barriers and redefine what strength looks like—physically, emotionally, and socially.

Vision

To become South Africa’s leading women-only fitness brand, redefining wellness through inclusivity and empowerment.

Our vision includes expanding into underserved communities, launching mobile wellness units, and creating leadership programs for young women who aspire to become fitness coaches and changemakers.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome women of all backgrounds, body types, and fitness levels.
  • Empowerment: We help women discover their inner and outer strength.
  • Community: We foster sisterhood and support through shared goals and experiences.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, respect, and authenticity.
